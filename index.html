Lynfield College

Address: 191 White Swan Road, Mount Roskill, Auckland, New Zealand

Total Students Enrolled: Around 2,000 students

Streams Offered: Junior School, Senior School, Sports, Arts, Technology, and Academic programmes

Educational Board: New Zealand Ministry of Education

Lynfield College is a diverse and well-known secondary school in Auckland. The school offers many opportunities in sports, music, culture, and learning.
